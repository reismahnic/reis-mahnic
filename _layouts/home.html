Who I Am

I'm a video game and software developer who loves exploring all forms of game design and interactive media. While working in film I began experimenting with nonlinear narrative and fell in love with storytelling in video games. Since then I've worked on a number of indie games in a variety of roles (most of which you can play from this site), studied and taught coding, and started my own development and publishing company, Side Group. Currently I am living in Seattle, where I work in AAA games while I continue to work on indie projects and support Side Group.
